ABOUT US

The Navkis College of Engineering has two separate boys and girls hostels which are furnished as per the needs of a student’s life. The hostels are located inside the campus.

Boys Hostel

The boys’ hostel is self-sufficient in all respects and provides double sharing, four sharing and five sharing accommodation with attached bathrooms with hot water facility. The hostel students are provided with student friendly menu. RO drinking water facility is available. The hostels are safe and with 24 hours security with CCTV surveillance. Inhouse wardens are responsible for the hostel and personal care is given a lot of importance. Indoor and outdoor sports facility is available. Health Centre is available in the campus.

Girl's Hostel

The Girl’s hostel is situated inside the campus. There are with double and triple room occupancy available. The hostel has dining hall, TV hall and indoor games facilities. The hostel students are provided with student friendly menu. It has 24 hours security with CCTV camera. The hostel has been provided with continual supply of hot water. The management has appointed a Warden (Residential) to take care of the day to day activities of the hostel. Cleanliness is given utmost importance.
